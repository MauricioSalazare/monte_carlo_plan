All the data comes from the station of Twente: Code 290.
Sources of the data:
1. https://www.knmi.nl/nederland-nu/klimatologie/uurgegevens (hourly resolution)
2. https://dataplatform.knmi.nl/ (10 min resolution)

The irradiance data at hourly resolution is in (J/cm^2)
The irradiance data at 10 min resolution is in (W/m^2)
